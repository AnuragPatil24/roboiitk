__WEEK 1:\- Gears, Motors, and Mechanisms__

### <a id="_v37womofkb7e"></a>__Objective:__ Time to dig into the nuts and bolts of robot movement\! Learn about gears, motors, and actuators—everything that makes your robot move, twist, and turn\. By the end, you’ll know exactly how to get your bot rolling without it falling flat on its face\.

#### <a id="_dusacorwsbea"></a>__Resources :\-__

1. __Gears — The Wheel Deal:__
	- [Types of Gears Explained \(and Why They’re More Than Just Spinning Discs\)](https://toolbox.igus.com/8515/types-of-gears)
	- Focus: Understand the different types of gears \(spur, bevel, worm—no, not the earthworm\), their applications, and how they influence speed and torque in your bot\.
2. __Motors — \(So Your Bot Doesn’t Just Sit There\):__
	- [Types of Motors Used in Robotics](https://robocraze.com/blogs/post/types-of-motors-used-in-robotics?srsltid=AfmBOor7ZhmZgBLCm5_IljRFKZg79_GM7rcRgokr9vhlJ-m4vwZToj1T)
	- Study: Learn about DC motors, stepper motors, and servos\. Each has a unique personality—pick the right one, and your bot will move like a dream\. Pick the wrong one, and… well, let’s just say things might get a bit jittery\.
3. __Choosing the Right Motor :__
	- [Motor Selection Guide](https://drive.google.com/file/d/105CuPGm6KznU1-lU8gB7wIvGDmhAnyhJ/view)
	- Key Points: Dive into torque, RPM, and power calculations\. Think of it as matchmaking for motors—find the one that fits your bot’s needs perfectly\.
4. __Actuators:__
	- [Robot Actuators Explained](https://elprocus.com/robot-actuator/)
	- Focus: Understand different actuators: linear, rotary, and pneumatic\. Imagine these as robotic gym equipment, giving your bot its mighty strength and flexibility\.
5. __Drive Mechanisms:__
	- [All About Robot Steering](https://kshitijtiwari.com/all-resources/mobile-robots/robot-steering/)
	- Recommended Reading: Learn about skid steering, Ackermann steering, differential drive, and holonomic drive\. It’s like choosing whether your bot wants to moonwalk or just zoom straight ahead\.
6. __Motor Drivers:__
	- [Types of Motor Drivers](https://robocraze.com/blogs/post/what-is-motor-driver?srsltid=AfmBOoranLLHNdeAB42ieIax_Q2SsluJm4dUs8LLQ7JDbBk97FB6yr3j)
	- Focus: Explore the L298N, H\-Bridge, and other drivers to ensure smooth control\. Without a good driver, your motor is just a fancy paperweight\.
7. __Motor Control Using L298N:__
	- [Controlling Two DC Motors Using L298N & Arduino](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dyjo_ggEtVU&ab_channel=DroneBotWorkshop)
	- Hands\-On: Follow along to control two DC motors using the L298N driver and Arduino\. This is where you get to make the motors dance\.
8. __CAD Design — Building Bots in the Digital World:__
	- [CAD Designing Playlist](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLmN15diDm2iKJ_snJ60QpUUMcte19QaTs)
	- Focus: Start learning Fusion 360 or your preferred CAD software to visualise and build robot parts before the real\-world chaos begins\.
